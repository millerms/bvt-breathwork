Jason Fitzgerald is an exercise physiologist and breathwork instructor trained
with Oxygen Advantage and Nordic Flow. He holds FMS, NFPT CPT, and Nordic Flow
cold plunge instructor certifications.

Jason’s coaching style is practical and evidence‑aware. He emphasizes nasal‑
dominant breathing, CO₂ tolerance work inspired by Buteyko practices, and
controlled cyclic breathing with breath holds. Sessions are paced to the person
in front of him. Intensity is titrated and opt‑outs are always offered.

No medical claims are made. For medical conditions, work with your clinician and
use breathwork as a supportive practice when appropriate.
