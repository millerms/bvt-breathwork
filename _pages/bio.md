Jason Fitzgerald is an exercise physiologist and breathwork instructor who’s trained with Oxygen Advantage and Nordic Flow. He holds certifications in FMS, NFPT CPT, and as a Nordic Flow cold plunge instructor.

Jason’s coaching is all about keeping things practical and grounded in solid evidence. He focuses on gentle, nasal breathing, building up CO₂ tolerance inspired by Buteyko methods, and using controlled breathing cycles with breath holds. Every session is tailored to fit you—paced just right, with intensity adjusted based on how you feel, and always giving you the freedom to opt out whenever you need.

While breathwork can be a helpful tool, Jason encourages working with your healthcare provider for any medical concerns and using breathwork as a supportive practice when it feels right for you.
