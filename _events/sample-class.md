What to expect:
- Gentle warm‑up and nasal breathing
- CO₂ tolerance practice (light, comfortable)
- Controlled cyclic breathing with optional, brief holds
- Plenty of recovery and down‑regulation

Who it’s for: Beginners to experienced practitioners who want a clear,
evidence‑aware foundation. Options are provided for all levels.

Accessibility and safety: Seated or lying positions are welcome. You can opt out
or slow down at any time. If you feel dizzy or uncomfortable, return to gentle
nasal breathing and rest.

Bring: Comfortable clothing, water, and a mat if you prefer lying practice.
